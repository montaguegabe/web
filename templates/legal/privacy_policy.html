Camera Roll Sounds Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Camera Roll Sounds helps you turn photos from your camera roll into guided audio meditations. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as your email address, authentication records, and profile information.
  • Photos you choose to upload so we can generate meditations and audio for you.
  • Generated meditation jobs, usage history, and related audio files.
  • Subscription and billing status needed to manage access to paid features.
  • Basic technical data such as device, browser, IP address, and diagnostic logs for security and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • Provide the core app experience, including photo processing and meditation generation.
  • Authenticate you, secure your account, and send account-related emails like verification messages.
  • Enforce usage limits, subscription access, and prevent abuse.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve Camera Roll Sounds.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the service.

Photos and Generated Content

We process only the photos you explicitly select. We do not scan your full photo library in the background. Uploaded photos and generated audio may be stored so you can access your results, troubleshoot issues, and support your account.

Payments and Subscriptions

Subscription access and related billing state may be handled by third-party payment providers. We store only the information needed to determine your subscription status and support your account. Payment card details are handled by the payment provider, not stored directly by Camera Roll Sounds.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, authentication, email delivery, analytics, and payment processing providers, or when required by law.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion by using the in-app delete account flow, which removes your account and associated access.

Your Choices

  • You can update account information from the app.
  • You can delete your account from the app.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy questions or request support.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact team@mindfulmakers.xyz.
